Frequently asked questions

Does VEGA work without an internet connection?

Yes. All core features — logging health data, viewing your pet's history, and receiving scheduled reminders — work entirely offline. Cloud sync is optional and additive; the app does not depend on it.

Where is my data stored?

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. If you choose to enable cloud sync, your data is backed up to our own servers hosted in the EU (Germany, via Hetzner). We do not use third-party cloud platforms like AWS or Google Cloud. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What is the AI insights feature and how do I opt in?

The AI insights feature analyses your pet's health logs to surface potential patterns — for example, early indicators of Chronic Kidney Disease in cats. It requires cloud sync to be enabled first, then you'll be asked separately whether you'd like to contribute anonymised data to improve the models.

Both consents are optional and independently revocable at any time in Settings → Data & Privacy.

AI insights are not diagnoses. They are suggestions to prompt a conversation with your vet.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This will permanently delete your account and trigger deletion of all your data from our servers within 30 days. On-device data is removed immediately when you uninstall the app.

If you need help with this, email us at privacy@vega-app.example.

How do I revoke my cloud sync or AI training consent?

Go to Settings → Data & Privacy. Both consent toggles are there and can be switched off independently at any time. Revoking cloud sync consent triggers server-side deletion of your data. Revoking AI training consent removes your anonymised data from our training pipeline.

My reminders aren't firing. What should I check?

Reminders are scheduled on-device and do not require internet. Check the following:

  • On Android: ensure VEGA has permission to send notifications and is not battery-optimised to the point of blocking background processes (Settings → Apps → VEGA → Battery → Unrestricted).
  • On iOS: ensure notifications are enabled for VEGA in Settings → Notifications → VEGA.
  • Check that the schedule is marked as active in Schedules within the app.

If the problem persists, please contact us with your device model and OS version.

Can I use VEGA for dogs or other pets?

VEGA launches with cats as the primary species, but the app is built to support multiple species. Dog support and further species are on the roadmap. When creating a pet profile, select the species that best matches your pet.

I've lost access to my account. How do I recover it?

Use the Forgot password link on the login screen to receive a password reset email. If you no longer have access to your registered email address, contact us at support@vega-app.example and we will help verify your identity.
