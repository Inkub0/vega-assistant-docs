Documentation

Getting started

From install to your first health log in a few minutes.

Installation

Download VEGA from the Google Play Store (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). Search for "VEGA Veterinary Guardian" or use the links on our home page.

VEGA requires Android 8.0+ or iOS 14+.

First launch

Choose how you'd like to use VEGA

You can use VEGA without an account — all data stays on your device. If you'd like to back up your data or sync across devices, tap Create account and enter your email address.

Add your first pet

Tap the + button on the Pets screen. Enter your pet's name, species, breed (optional), date of birth, sex, and whether they are neutered. Add a photo if you like. Tap Save.

Decide on cloud sync (optional)

After adding your first pet, VEGA will ask whether you want to enable cloud sync. This backs up your data to our EU servers and lets you access it across devices. You can say no and change your mind later in Settings.

Start logging

Tap your pet's name to open their profile, then tap Add log to record your first health entry. See Logging health data for what you can record.

Finding your way around

Pets screen

Your home screen lists all your pets. Tap a pet to open their profile. Tap + to add a new pet.

Pet profile

Shows your pet's details, recent log entries, and quick-access buttons for logging and reminders. Use the three-dot menu (⋮) to edit the profile or delete the pet.

Logs

A full chronological history of health entries for a pet. Scroll to review past logs or use the filter to focus on a specific type (e.g. weight only).

Schedules

All feeding and medication reminders for a pet. Tap a schedule to edit it, or use the toggle to pause it without deleting it.

Insights

AI-generated pattern summaries (requires cloud sync + AI training consent). See AI insights for details.

Settings

Manage your account, consent choices, notification preferences, and app behaviour.

Tip: VEGA works fully offline. Reminders will still fire, and you can keep logging even without a connection. Any unsynced entries will upload automatically the next time you're online (if sync is enabled).
