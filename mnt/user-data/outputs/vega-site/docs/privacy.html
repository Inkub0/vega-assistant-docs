Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Plain-language summary: VEGA stores your data on your device by default. Cloud sync and AI training are both opt-in, separate, and revocable at any time. We do not sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. All cloud data is stored on our own servers in the EU.

1. Who we are

VEGA (Veterinary Guardian Assistant) is a mobile application developed and operated by [Your Name / Company Name] ("we", "us", "our"). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@vega-app.example.

2. What data we collect

2a. Data you provide

  • Pet profiles: name, species, breed, date of birth, sex, neutered status, and optionally a photo.
  • Health logs: weight entries, appetite notes, medication records, mood observations, and any custom notes you add.
  • Schedules: feeding times and medication reminders you configure.
  • Account information (cloud sync only): your email address, used solely for authentication if you choose to enable cloud sync.

2b. Data collected automatically

  • Crash reports: anonymous crash logs may be collected to help us fix bugs. These contain no pet or health data.
  • App usage diagnostics: basic non-identifiable usage signals (e.g. app version, OS version) may be collected to improve stability.

We do not collect location data, contacts, or any data unrelated to your pet care activity.

3. How your data is stored

3a. On-device storage (default)

All data you enter is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (see Section 4).

3b. Cloud storage (opt-in)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is uploaded to and stored on our own private servers hosted by Hetzner Online GmbH, located in the European Union (Germany). We operate this infrastructure ourselves — your data is not stored on third-party cloud platforms such as AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Data at rest is encrypted on the server.

4. Your consent choices

VEGA operates on two independent, separately granted consent tiers. Neither is required to use the app.

Consent 1 — Cloud sync

When you first register, we ask whether you want your data backed up and synced across devices. If you agree:

  • Your pet profiles, health logs, and schedules are synced to our EU servers.
  • Your email address is stored for authentication purposes only.
  • You can revoke this consent at any time in Settings → Data & Privacy. Revoking triggers immediate deletion of your data from our servers.

Consent 2 — AI training

We ask this separately, and only after cloud sync is enabled. If you agree:

  • A fully anonymised copy of your pet's health logs (no name, no account link, no pet name — only species and log data) may be used to improve our disease-detection models.
  • This consent is independent of cloud sync consent. You can revoke it at any time without affecting your sync.
  • Revoking AI training consent triggers deletion of your anonymised data from our training datasets.
Both consents are optional. Revoking either one is immediate and always available in Settings. We will never bundle or pre-tick these consents.

5. How we use your data

  • To operate the app and provide the features you use.
  • To sync your data across your devices (cloud sync consent required).
  • To power AI-assisted health insights for your pet (AI training consent required — only anonymised data is used).
  • To send push notifications for reminders you have scheduled.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating VEGA.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Hetzner Online GmbH: our hosting provider, operating under EU data protection law. They process data only as a data processor on our instruction.
  • Legal obligation: if required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business transfer: in the event of a merger or acquisition, we will notify users and ensure the same privacy protections apply.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw either consent tier at any time without penalty.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vega-app.example. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the UK, that is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

8. Data retention

  • On-device data: retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data: retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion or consent revocation.
  • Anonymised AI training data: deleted from training datasets within 30 days of AI training consent revocation.
  • Crash logs: retained for 90 days.

9. Children's privacy

VEGA is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice before the change takes effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.
