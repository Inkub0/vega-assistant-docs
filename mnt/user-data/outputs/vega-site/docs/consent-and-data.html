Consent & your data

What we collect, what you control, and how to manage or delete your data.

Your two consent choices

VEGA has two independent opt-in features. Neither is required to use the app. Both can be revoked at any time.

Optional

Cloud sync

Backs up your pet data and syncs it across your devices. Your data is stored on our own servers in the EU (Germany). Your email address is used for authentication only.

To enable: Settings → Data & Privacy → Cloud sync → On
To revoke: Settings → Data & Privacy → Cloud sync → Off
Revoking triggers deletion of your data from our servers within 30 days.

Optional — requires cloud sync

AI training

Contributes fully anonymised health log data to improve VEGA's disease detection models. No name, no account link, no pet name — only species and log data. This is presented separately from cloud sync and is never pre-ticked.

To enable: Settings → Data & Privacy → AI training → On
To revoke: Settings → Data & Privacy → AI training → Off
Revoking removes your anonymised data from our training pipeline within 30 days.

Exporting your data

Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export my data. VEGA will generate a JSON file containing all your pet profiles and health logs. You can save this to your device or share it.

Deleting your data

Delete a single log entry

Tap the entry in the Logs list, then tap the delete icon. The entry is removed from your device immediately. If cloud sync is on, it is removed from our servers within 24 hours.

Delete a pet

Open the pet's profile, tap the three-dot menu (⋮), and select Delete pet. Confirm in the prompt. This deletes the pet profile, all their health logs, and all their schedules — from your device immediately and from our servers within 24 hours.

Delete your account

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account and confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data from our servers within 30 days. On-device data is removed when you uninstall the app.

If you need help deleting your account, email privacy@vega-app.example — we are required to process your request under GDPR.

Your GDPR rights

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, correct, erase, or port your data, and to object to or restrict its processing. Contact us at privacy@vega-app.example to exercise any of these rights. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
